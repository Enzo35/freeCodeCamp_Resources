About Alan Turing

Alan Turing (1912-1954) was born in the city of Londres, and studied at:

  • The Universety of Manchester
  • The Government Code and Cypher School
  • The National Physical Laboratory

Alan turing is considered by many the Founder of Computer Science

Accomplishments

  • Decoded the encryption of German Enigma machines;
  • Forming the basis for AI Tecnology, by creating a procedure called The Turing Test;
  • And his work on the developing of the first modern computers
